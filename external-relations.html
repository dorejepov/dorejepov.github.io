Finding Potential Investors

Finding the right investors for our club involves utilizing various platforms and networks. Here are some resources where you can start your search:

  • LinkedIn - Professional networking site where you can connect with industry leaders and investors.
  • AngelList - A platform for startups, angel investors, and job-seekers looking to work at startups.
  • Meetup - Find and join local networking events and investor meetups relevant to real estate and entrepreneurship.
  • Crunchbase - For researching and connecting with venture capitalists and private equity firms.
