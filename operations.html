Operations Department

Master Calendar - Fall Semester 2024

    Semester Schedule

    Week 1 (Aug 28 - Sep 3)

    • Club Introduction Meeting: Host an inaugural meeting to introduce the club, its mission, and the semester's activities.
    • Begin Recruitment: Launch recruitment drive with sign-up sheets and promotional materials.

    Week 2 (Sep 4 - Sep 10)

    • Marketing Blitz: Start aggressive marketing campaign using flyers, social media, and campus posters.
    • First Guest Speaker Event: Organize an event with a local real estate professional.

    Week 3 (Sep 11 - Sep 17)

    • Membership Finalization: Close initial recruitment phase.
    • Workshop 1: Host a workshop on "Basics of Real Estate Investment."

    Week 4 (Sep 18 - Sep 24)

    • Operations Meeting: Review event feedback and plan adjustments for upcoming events.
    • Team Building Activity: For club members to enhance collaboration.

    Week 5 (Sep 25 - Oct 1)

    • Marketing Review: Analyze the effectiveness of the marketing strategies and adjust as necessary.
    • Fundraiser Planning Begins: Start planning for a mid-semester fundraiser.

    Week 6 (Oct 2 - Oct 8)

    • Real Estate Tour: Arrange a tour of local real estate properties or developments.
    • Finance Update Meeting: Review financial status and fundraising goals.

    Week 7 (Oct 9 - Oct 15)

    • Mid-Semester Review: Assess progress and member engagement.
    • Prepare for Fundraiser: Finalize plans and roles for the fundraiser event.

    Week 8 (Oct 16 - Oct 22)

    • Fundraiser Event: Execute the planned fundraising event.
    • Post-Event Debrief: Gather feedback and discuss outcomes in a follow-up meeting.

    Week 9 (Oct 23 - Oct 29)

    • Guest Speaker 2: Invite another industry expert, focus on commercial real estate.
    • Research & Development Presentation: Showcase new learning resources or tools.

    Week 10 (Oct 30 - Nov 5)

    • External Relations Workshop: Host a workshop on networking and professional growth in real estate.
    • Operations Strategy Session: Plan for end-of-semester activities and next semester's preliminary ideas.

    Week 11 (Nov 6 - Nov 12)

    • Entrepreneurship Event: Small business pop-up event featuring local real estate startups.
    • Marketing Push for Final Events: Increase visibility for end-of-semester events.

    Week 12 (Nov 13 - Nov 19)

    • Membership Drive for Next Semester: Start early recruitment for the Spring semester.
    • Professional Development Workshop: Focus on career opportunities in real estate.

    Week 13 (Nov 20 - Nov 26)

    • Thanksgiving Break: No scheduled events. Use this time for planning and regrouping.

    Week 14 (Nov 27 - Dec 3)

    • End of Semester Gala: Organize a formal event to celebrate the semester's successes and network.
    • Feedback Collection: Distribute surveys to gather member feedback on the club's activities and impact.

    Week 15 (Dec 4 - Dec 14)

    • Final Exams Period: No major events planned.
    • Planning for Next Semester: Begin detailed planning for Spring 2025 based on feedback and this semester's learnings.
