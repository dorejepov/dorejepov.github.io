Human Resources Department

Ensuring fair roles and harmonious relationships within our club.

Human Resources - UC Merced Real Estate Club

Role Assignment

Our department is dedicated to clearly defining roles and responsibilities within the club to ensure effective management and fair workload distribution. This includes:

  • Assigning specific tasks based on individual strengths and career interests.
  • Regularly reviewing roles to adapt to changing needs and member development.
  • Providing clear descriptions and expectations for each position to avoid overlaps and gaps in responsibilities.

Conflict Resolution

To maintain a harmonious working environment, our HR team has developed strategies for effective conflict resolution, including:

  • Implementing a clear process for reporting and addressing grievances within the club.
  • Facilitating mediation sessions to resolve conflicts amicably and constructively.
  • Conducting workshops on communication and conflict management skills for all members.
