Research & Development Department

Innovating and expanding knowledge within the realm of real estate.

Research Areas of Interest

Emerging Real Estate Markets

  • Objective: To understand the dynamics and potential of newly developing real estate markets, both domestically and internationally. This includes studying market trends, investment opportunities, and economic impacts.
  • Rationale: As real estate markets evolve, identifying and analyzing emerging markets will provide valuable insights for investors and policymakers, ensuring our members are well-prepared for diverse real estate environments.

Technology in Real Estate

  • Objective: Explore how cutting-edge technologies like artificial intelligence, blockchain, and virtual reality are transforming the real estate industry, from property management to sales processes.
  • Rationale: Technology is reshaping how properties are bought, sold, and managed. Understanding these technologies can offer our students a competitive edge in the job market and empower them to innovate within the industry.

Environmental Impact Studies

  • Objective: Investigate the environmental impacts of real estate developments, focusing on sustainable building practices and their efficacy in reducing carbon footprints.
  • Rationale: With growing concerns over climate change and environmental sustainability, this research area will prepare students to lead the way in sustainable real estate solutions, promoting developments that harmonize with ecological health.

Goals for the Department

Establish Research Projects

  • Goal: Initiate and develop structured research projects within the identified areas of interest. These projects will serve as the backbone for academic exploration and student involvement.
  • Action Plan: Begin with pilot studies that can be scaled up, seeking collaboration with faculty and industry experts to ensure practical relevance and academic rigor.

Develop Partnerships

  • Goal: Forge partnerships with real estate firms, tech companies, and environmental organizations. These partnerships will enhance research opportunities and provide practical training environments for students.
  • Action Plan: Identify potential partners who align with our research interests, and establish formal relationships through joint projects, internships, and sponsorships.

Enhance Academic Curriculum

  • Goal: Leverage research findings to enrich the real estate academic curriculum, integrating hands-on learning experiences that reflect current industry standards and future trends.
  • Action Plan: Work closely with academic staff to periodically review and update course offerings and materials, ensuring they include the latest research insights and technological advancements.
