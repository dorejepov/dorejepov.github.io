International Property Buying Guide

Top Countries to Buy Property Internationally

Here is a guide to buying property in various countries, including top websites for property listings and key steps to follow in each country.

1. USA

Websites: Zillow, Realtor

Steps: Find a property, hire a real estate agent, get pre-approved for a mortgage, make an offer, conduct inspections, close the deal.

2. China

Websites: Anjuke, Fang.com

Steps: Hire a local real estate agent, confirm foreign ownership eligibility, arrange financing, sign contracts, register the property.

3. Mexico

Websites: Inmuebles24, Lamudi

Steps: Hire a local agent, secure a fideicomiso (trust) if near the coast, conduct property inspection, sign contract, register the deed.

4. Canada

Websites: Realtor, Zillow Canada

Steps: Find a property, work with a real estate agent, get financing, make an offer, conduct inspections, close the sale.

5. France

Websites: SeLoger, Green-Acres

Steps: Find a property, sign a preliminary contract, arrange financing, hire a notaire, complete the purchase.

6. Germany

Websites: ImmobilienScout24, Immowelt

Steps: Find a property, hire a notary, arrange financing, sign the purchase contract, register ownership.

7. Turkey

Websites: Sahibinden, Hurriyet Emlak

Steps: Find a property, check property eligibility, secure financing, sign the contract, register with the land registry.

8. UAE

Websites: Property Finder, Bayut

Steps: Find a property, check foreign ownership zones, arrange financing, sign the purchase contract, complete the registration.

9. Saudi Arabia

Websites: Aqar, Bayut

Steps: Consult with a local real estate agent, confirm foreign ownership eligibility, arrange financing, sign the contract, complete registration.

10. Australia

Websites: Realestate.com.au, Domain

Steps: Obtain Foreign Investment Review Board (FIRB) approval, find a property, secure financing, conduct due diligence, complete the purchase.

11. Japan

Websites: Suumo, Lifull Homes

Steps: Hire a local agent, confirm financing, conduct due diligence, sign the contract, register the property.

12. Italy

Websites: Immobiliare.it, Casa.it

Steps: Find a property, hire a notary, secure financing, sign preliminary contract, finalize sale with notary.
