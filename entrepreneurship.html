Entrepreneurship Department

Fundraiser Plan

Identity

The fundraiser will feature a "Future of Real Estate" theme with modern, sustainable designs and digital interactions to highlight innovation.

Problem

Key risks include low attendance, budget constraints, and potential conflicts with other events.

Our Solution

Solutions include early and aggressive marketing, backup plans for logistics, and flexible budgeting with contingency funds.

Target Market

The target audience includes real estate students, local professionals, faculty, and investors interested in innovative real estate developments.

The Competition

The event will differentiate itself with exclusive workshops, high-profile speakers, and student project showcases.

Revenue Streams

Income will be generated through ticket sales, sponsorships, and a silent auction of donated items.

Marketing Activities

Promotion will occur via social media, email newsletters, and strategic placements of posters and banners across campus.

Expenses

Major expenses will include venue rental, speaker fees, marketing materials, and catering, divided into short-term and long-term categories.

Team and Key Roles

The team will consist of roles like Project Manager, Marketing Director, Sponsorship Coordinator, and Logistics Manager, requiring significant commitment in the lead-up to the event.

Milestones

Goals include raising $5,000, achieving a 50% profit margin, attracting 200 attendees, and engaging actively on social media.
